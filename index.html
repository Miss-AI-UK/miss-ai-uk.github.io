Bletchley Park Auditorium

Bletchley Park is the home of the codebreakers from WWII, including Alan Turing. It is renowned as the place AI was conceived.

They hosted the first AI Safety Summit in November 2023 attended by world leaders and tech leaders in November 2023.

This is an opportunity to learn about the next generation of AI in one of the most important places in AI history.
